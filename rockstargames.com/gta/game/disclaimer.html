BMG INTERACTIVE COMPETITION RULES.

These rules apply to all Competitions included on the BMG Interactive website, unless otherwise expressly stated.

  1. No employee of BMG Interactive or its subsidiary or associated companies, or their agents or members of their families or households shall be eligible to enter.
  2. No employee of any company which has made or produced any game to which a competition relates shall be eligible to enter any competition for which a prize is offered.
  3. In the event of any dispute regarding the rules, conduct, or the results of any Competition, the decision of BMG Interactive shall be final.
  4. All prizes shall be awarded at the discretion of the BMG Interactive Web Site Editor.
  5. No prizes will be awarded as a result of improper actions by or on behalf of contestants, illegal or otherwise.
  6. Where a prize may not be appropriate for a younger contestant, a minimum age for entry will be stated and must be observed. BMG Interactive reserves the right to request written proof of age of any winner.
  7. No cash or other alternatives will be offered in place of specified prizes.
  8. Winners of competitions will be notified by e-mail and their name will be published on the BMG Interactive website. Claims for prizes must be made within the specified time. Failure to claim by the specified time will make the claim invalid.
  9. BMG Interactive reserves the right to use the names and addresses of winners, their photographs, and audio or visual recordings of them in any publicity.
  10. This is the full set of rules relating to competitions on the BMG Interactive website.
  11. BMG Interactive reserves the right to stop a competition and/or vary these rules at any time, without prior notice. Any changes will be included in the rules posted within the website.
  12. These rules shall be governed by the laws of England.
